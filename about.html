About me

Hey there! I’m Carlos, App Developer & Graphic Designer.

For as long as I can remember, drawing has become my main attraction. My studies as a designer allowed me to specialise in editorial projects and signage.
I had a great passion for infographics and data, which helped me to create my first commercial project called “Harry Potter: a través de los mapas”. I am currently working on other personal projects, considering them as a hobby at the moment.

From web design I got to know programming, which allowed me to open myself to more complete and enriching projects, such as applications and webs (and videogames sometimes).

More than 5 years of experience in communication agency specialising in web design, infographics, editorial & branding and 3 years of experience in App Development Agency. When the work is fruitful, the exigency for details keeps me awake at night. If there is a good connection, I like to work as a team.
